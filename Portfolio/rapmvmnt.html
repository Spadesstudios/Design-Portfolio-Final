Overview

More Than a Brand — A Movement

Rapmvmnt (read: "Rap Movement") was built as a cultural artifact — an apparel brand that exists at the intersection of hip-hop culture, visual art, and community identity. It's not just clothing; it's a statement of belonging, of movement, of shared values expressed through what you wear.

The intentional stylization of the name — dropping the vowel from "movement" — is itself a design choice: compact, typographically interesting, and immediately distinguishable. Everything about Rapmvmnt is intentional.

"Apparel is wearable design. Every piece we put out is a graphic design project that someone carries with them into the world."

Brand Strategy

Identity Built on Culture

Most apparel brands start with a product. Rapmvmnt started with a culture. The brand strategy was rooted in understanding who the community was, what they stood for, and how to create visual language that reflected and amplified their identity.

  • Developed brand positioning that sits at the intersection of hip-hop culture and streetwear aesthetics
  • Defined the brand's cultural ethos: authenticity, expression, community, and forward motion
  • Built a content strategy that led with culture and community — not just product drops
  • Designed community engagement mechanics: artist collaborations, local events, and brand ambassador program
  • Created brand partnership framework for collaboration with artists, photographers, and creatives
Graphic Design

Visual Language & Design System

Graphic design is the core product at Rapmvmnt. Every piece of apparel starts with a visual concept — a graphic, a typographic treatment, a statement. Building a cohesive visual language across seasons and drops requires the same design systems thinking you'd apply to a digital product.

01
Typography First
Custom type treatments and hand-lettered elements distinguish each collection and create ownable typographic moments.
02
Bold color stories that feel culturally current but timelessly wearable — built collection-by-collection with coherent palettes.
Color Intentionality
03
Graphic Narrative
Every graphic tells a story. From concept sketches to print-ready artwork, each design references the culture it's speaking to.
04
System Thinking
Collections designed as cohesive sets — individual pieces work alone, but come alive when worn together as a system.

Technical design work included:

  • Full apparel graphics — original vector artwork, typography, and print-ready files
  • Lookbook photography direction and layout design
  • Hang tag, label, and packaging design
  • Digital and social campaign asset creation
  • E-commerce storefront design and product photography direction
Operations

From Design to Delivery

Running an apparel business requires managing a complex supply chain — from design through manufacturing, quality control, inventory, fulfillment, and customer experience. I built and managed the full operation.

  • Managed full print-on-demand and custom manufacturing pipeline
  • Vendor sourcing for blank garments, printing, and embroidery
  • Inventory management and drop release strategy to create demand and scarcity
  • E-commerce platform setup, product pages, and checkout experience optimization
  • Customer experience design from purchase through delivery — including packaging and unboxing
Marketing

Movement-Led Marketing

Rapmvmnt's marketing philosophy is grounded in authenticity over advertising. Rather than paying for attention, the brand earns it by being genuinely embedded in the culture it serves — showing up at events, collaborating with artists, and producing content that people want to share because it resonates, not because it's promoted.

  • Social content strategy built around community storytelling and cultural moments
  • Drop release campaigns that built anticipation and community engagement pre-launch
  • Artist and photographer collaboration for organic brand exposure
  • Event activations at community and music events for physical brand presence
  • Email and SMS marketing flows for direct-to-consumer relationship building
